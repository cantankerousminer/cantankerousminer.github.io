BankNode

fun BankNode(startTile: WorldTile, itemsToWithdraw: List<RequiredItem>, itemsToDeposit: List<RequiredItem>, action: String, depositEquipment: Boolean = false, depositAll: Boolean = true)

Parameters

startTile

The tile near which a bank can be found.

itemsToWithdraw

A list of item RequiredItem to withdraw from the bank. When authoring quest nodes, provide a list of item ids and their corresponding quantities.

itemsToDeposit

A list of item RequiredItem to deposit into the bank. All are deposited; quantity is ignored. When authoring quest nodes, provide a list of item ids.

action

The action to take on the bank object to open the bank e.g. "Open" or "Bank"

depositEquipment

Whether to deposit all worn equipment or not.

depositAll

Whether to deposit all items or not. Will deposit equipment only if depositEquipment is also true.
